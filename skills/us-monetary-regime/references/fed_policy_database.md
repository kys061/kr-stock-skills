## Policy Keywords Reference

### Hawkish Keywords
inflation, elevated, restrictive, vigilant, tighten, upside risks,
data-dependent (when used with caution), remains high, further tightening,
price stability, above target, labor market tight, wage pressures,
persistent, sticky, not yet sufficient, premature

### Dovish Keywords
support, accommodate, easing, downside risk, labor market softening,
progress toward target, balance of risks, sufficient progress,
well positioned, gradual, appropriate, normalization, restrictive enough,
cooling, moderating, disinflation, confident, sustainable path

### Neutral Keywords
monitoring, assessing, evolving, balanced, both sides, uncertain outlook,
data will guide, meeting by meeting, no preset course
